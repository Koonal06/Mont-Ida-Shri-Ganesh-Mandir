About Us

Mont-Ida Shri Ganesh Mandir is a vibrant community center preserving Marathi culture and devotion in Mauritius. We celebrate festivals such as Ganesh Chaturthi and Navratri, and organize cultural programs to bring the community together.

Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for prayer, cultural learning, and community bonding while embracing modern technology to share activities with the wider community.

Our Values

  • Faith and Devotion
  • Community and Togetherness
  • Cultural Preservation
  • Inclusivity and Respect
