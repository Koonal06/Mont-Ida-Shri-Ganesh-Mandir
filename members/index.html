Leadership And Service

The people and roles that help the temple flourish

This page highlights the leadership structure and committee support that keep the temple welcoming, organized, and community-focused.

Leadership Team

Structured to serve the community with care

This page highlights the leadership roles and shared responsibilities that help the temple stay welcoming, organized, and connected to the wider community.

P
President

Guides the vision of the temple community

The president helps shape long-term direction, supports major festival planning, and ensures the temple continues to serve its spiritual and cultural purpose with clarity.

Why This Matters

Leadership with shared responsibility

The temple's leadership structure helps major celebrations, communication, finances, and community support remain clear and well coordinated throughout the year.

VP
Vice President

Supports planning and coordination

Helps oversee festivals, day-to-day operations, and the smooth running of temple activities.

S
Secretary

Keeps communication and records organized

Supports announcements, meeting records, and the communication that keeps the community informed.

T
Treasurer

Protects accountability and financial care

Oversees budgeting, donations, and responsible financial support for temple programs and events.

Committee Support

Shared responsibilities across the wider team

The strength of the mandir comes from many helping hands. These support roles reflect the kind of teamwork that keeps the temple welcoming and active.

FC

Festival Coordination

Helping prepare spaces, schedules, and celebrations for major temple events.

VO

Volunteer Organization

Bringing people together to support logistics, hospitality, and service.

CP

Cultural Programs

Encouraging music, dance, youth learning, and community participation.

CO

Community Outreach

Helping the temple stay open, approachable, and connected to the wider community.
